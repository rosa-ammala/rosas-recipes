Banana Mousse

Published on:

3.11.2024

Categories:

Desserts

Cooking time:

15 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar (for whipped cream)
  • Sliced bananas or chocolate shavings for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a blender or food processor, combine the ripe bananas, granulated sugar, lemon juice, and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth and creamy
  2. In a separate large bowl, whip the heavy cream until soft peaks form. Gradually add the powdered sugar and continue to whip until stiff peaks form
  3. Gently fold the banana mixture into the whipped cream until well incorporated. Be careful not to deflate the whipped cream
  4. Spoon the banana mousse into serving glasses or bowls and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until set
  5. Before serving, garnish with sliced bananas or chocolate shavings if desired. Enjoy your creamy and delicious banana mousse!
